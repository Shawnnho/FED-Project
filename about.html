What is HawkerPoint?

HawkerPoint is a digital platform designed to support Singapore's hawker ecosystem by connecting customers, stallholders, and authorities in one unified system.

What can users do?

Customers can discover hawker stalls, view menus, hygiene grades, and leave feedback. Stallholders manage stalls and menus, while authorities oversee hygiene inspections and compliance.

Our goal

To modernise hawker centres while preserving their cultural significance and improving transparency.
