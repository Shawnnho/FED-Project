How It Works

Simple steps for customers, stallholders, and NEA officers


Overview

HawkerConnect connects customers, stallholders, and authorities in one platform. Below is a quick workflow for each user role.

For Customers

1) Browse hawker centres and stalls

2) View menus, hygiene grades, and reviews

3) Place orders (if enabled) or leave feedback

For Stallholders

1) Register a stall and complete stall details

2) Add and manage menu items (name, price, availability)

3) Track customer feedback and stall performance

For NEA Officers / Admin

1) Record inspection outcomes

2) Assign or update hygiene grades

3) Monitor compliance and trends across centres

Transparency & Trust

Hygiene grades are shown for public awareness. This encourages good hygiene practices while helping customers make informed choices.
