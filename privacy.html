Privacy Policy

How we collect, use, and protect your data

Overview

This Privacy Policy explains what data we collect, why we collect it, how it is used, and what choices you have.

Last updated: 1 February 2026

1. Information We Collect

  • Account details (e.g., email, role)
  • Profile info (e.g., stall name, unit number) for stallholders
  • Content you submit (e.g., reviews, feedback, images)

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To provide platform features (menus, centres, stall management)
  • To improve safety and trust (hygiene grade display, moderation)
  • To respond to support requests

3. Sharing of Information

We only share data when necessary to operate the platform (e.g., displaying stall info, reviews) or when required by law.

4. Data Storage

Data is stored securely using Firebase services. Access is controlled through authentication and security rules.

5. Your Choices

  • You may update your profile details
  • You may remove content you submitted (where allowed)
  • You may request help via the Contact page

Contact

If you have questions about this policy, contact us.
