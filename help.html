Need help?

Start by searching below, or tap a category to filter. If you still need assistance, reach us via Contact Us.

Click Sign Up and choose your role (Guest, Customer, Stallholder). After signing up, you'll be brought to the correct dashboard.
Double-check your email and password. If you forgot your password, use the password recovery option on the login page.
Go to Centres, choose a hawker centre, then select a stall to view its menu, details, and hygiene grade.
After placing an order, go to your Orders page to view the latest status and details.
After your experience, submit feedback from the feedback/review section. Reviews help stallholders improve and help other customers decide.
Use your Stallholder dashboard to update stall information and menu items. Changes help customers see your latest offerings.
Go to your dashboard/orders section to see new orders and manage their progress.
Inspections may be scheduled in advance. After an inspection is completed, hygiene results can be recorded and shown for public awareness.
Hygiene grades help customers make informed decisions. A better grade generally indicates stronger hygiene standards.
Payments are made during checkout. Always review your order and total amount before confirming.
